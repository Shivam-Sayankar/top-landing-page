Explore the Four Core Types
Clicky Switches
Experience the classic, satisfying 'click' sound and a distinct tactile bump with every press, offering audible and physical feedback.
Linear Switches
Enjoy a smooth, consistent keystroke from top to bottom, without any tactile bump or audible click, ideal for rapid presses.
Tactile Switches
Feel a noticeable bump during actuation, providing feedback without the loud 'click,' perfect for typing accuracy and silent gaming
Hall Effect Switches
Magnetic sensing offers extreme precision, durability, and often adjustable actuation, redefining speed and control.
